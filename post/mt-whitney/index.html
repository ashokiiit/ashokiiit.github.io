Mt.Whitney, CA

Elevation: 14,505 ft (4,421 m)

Location: Inyo National Forest, Sequoia.

Dates: April27th-30th, 2016.

After relaxing for 2 week-ends after Nepal Trip, time is back to hit California mountains.

Attempted Mt.Whitney via Mountaineers Route with friends from Sierra Mountaineering Club. Freezing temps, stuck in tent for 14hrs and able to reach upto 12,500ft (Check point - Iceberg Lake). An alaskan alpine experience in Sierras.

Mt.Thor(12,306ft) when returning back.
