Matterhorn Peak, CA

Elevation: 12,280ft (3,744 m)

Location: Hoover Wilderness, North Yosemite.

Dates: May4th-5th, 2016.

Rob Ritchey from Sierra Mountaineering club organized free expedition training (MTN3), to climb Matterhorn Peak, Hoover wilderness, CA. As you can see from the pic, Matterhorn Peak (12279 ft / 3743 m) has East and West Couloir. Instead of going through the Main ridgeline (on the left), or over the glacier plateau, we did an amazing spicy traverse on the right to reach the base of west colouir. Knee deep snow and white-out, so we didn’t climb it and ended at 11,300ft.

Spicy traverse on North-west walls.
