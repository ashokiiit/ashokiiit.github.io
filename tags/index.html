Matterhorn Peak, CA

Elevation: 12,280ft (3,744 m) Location: Hoover Wilderness, Toyabe National Forest. Dates: May4th-5th, 2016. Group: Sierra Mountaineering Club. Tags: Mountaineering, Steep Traverse Rob Ritchey from Sierra Mountaineering club organized free expedition training (MTN3), to climb Matterhorn Peak, Hoover wilderness, CA. As you can see from the pic, Matterhorn Peak (12279 ft / 3743 m) has East and West Couloir. Instead of going through the Main ridgeline (on the left), or over the glacier plateau, we did an amazing spicy traverse on the right to reach the base of west colouir.

Mt.Whitney - 2016

Attempted Mt.Whitney via Mountaineers Route with friends from Sierra Mountaineering Club. Freezing temps, stuck in tent for 14hrs and able to reach upto 12,500ft (Iceberg Lake). An alaskan alpine experience in California Sierras.
